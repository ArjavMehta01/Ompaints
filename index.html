About Us

A Certified and Entrusted Dealer representation in terms of Trading Paints and Allied items.

Our Involvement

With being involved in many Commercial and Residential Projects of Varied Scale, to supplying to Associations, Hotels, Maintenance Contractors we have learned a lot and are hopeful to adapt to newer trends.

Our Aim

The aim now has moved from expanding vertically to growing horizontally. With extremely comfortable relations with Leading Contractors and Paint Manufacturing Companies we tend to become one of the best and brightest possibility of business.

Variety for the Customer

We tend to decorative Consumption, exterior projects and also Nitro Cellulose range. With PU finish and Wood Coatings, we try to make a large variety available to the customer.We believe that serving the customer remains our prior aim and we would like to enhance their experience while doing so.

What we do

We at Om, have developed a keen understanding on Market Dynamics of Paint to be used, or to be sold. Since inception, trading being our core business, we have learnt a lot about customer needs, and results. To providing the right paint in required shades is our forte. To create a mutual understanding of business for cohesive growth remains our target.

Now to create another approach, we also tend to application of Paint and Décor Materials along with assistance from Architects and Interior Designers to help customers create their own designs.We do not believe in one time sale, we believe in building a relationship with all our customers.

Our Future Plan

Through expansion in our Internal Structure, we have begun providing tips and guidance on works through Paint Manufacturing Companies. We also tend into the Customer ourselves with our knowledge and backing to make the same process better and more efficient.

We are also working on a new module of DIY, for the new generation artists.
